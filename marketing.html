Marketing Analytics Dashboard

SQL | Power BI | Customer Segmentation | RFM Analysis

Business Problem

The company lacked clear visibility into customer purchasing behavior and marketing campaign effectiveness. Marketing decisions were not data-driven, leading to inefficient targeting and lower campaign response rates. There was a need to identify high-value customers and improve segmentation strategy.

Key Insights

High-value customers contribute a major portion of total revenue but are not consistently targeted in campaigns. Middle-aged customers show the highest engagement and response rates. Senior customers generate strong revenue but have lower interaction with campaigns. Customer segmentation using RFM analysis revealed clear differences in behavior and value contribution.

KPIs

Total Customers, Total Revenue, Average Order Value, Campaign Response Rate, High-Value Customers (Champions), Revenue at Risk, Customer Retention Rate

Tools Used

SQL, Power BI, Excel, RFM Analysis
